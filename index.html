Kiwe


Kiwe is a Twitter style social media website that I made using NodeJS, Express, Pug and MongoDB. Features include:

  • Chirping (Posting) including likes, replies and retweets.

  • Following

  • Realtime Notifications

  • Realtime messaging including typing notifications.

  • Profile pages including pinning chirps, profile pictures, banners and bios.

  • Search page including users and chirps.

  • Admin panel including ability to verify users, governments and brands; add debug likes to posts.

  • Still under development, adding trending, image chirps, and inline advert chirps.

BlockBandit (WIP)


Blockbandit is an under development crypto game I am working on with another CS student, Dima Galkin. BlockBandit will be a crypto game where players can complete various digital heists and challenges to 'steal' tokens from other players. These tokens can then be used to fortify your base and make it harder for others to steal from you.


Blockbandit will be hosted on the Ethereum network and thus is coded in the Ethereum network's language solidity.


We are currently targeting a token pre-sale mid way through 2024 with a full launch towards the end of the same year.
